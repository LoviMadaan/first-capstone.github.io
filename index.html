Pizza Cafe's

International Pizza Expo

2023

Now in its 39th year, the annual International Pizza Expo remains unchallenged as the industry’s leading event. Join thousands of pizzeria professionals as they convene in New Delhi Convention Center on April 06-08, 2023 for 3 days dedicated to all things pizza. Suppliers from across the globe, educational sessions taught by top industry professionals, and national competitions, this event is one you simply cannot afford to miss!

2023.04.06(THU) ~ 08(sat)

@ New Delhi Convention Center.

Main Program

  • WORLD PIZZA GAMES

    Beginning with the Individual Event Trials on Tuesday and ending with the Finals on Wednesday evening at the Block Party, the World Pizza Games competition brings energy and excitement to the Pizza Expo show floor. The best pizzaiolo is crowned in each division of the five divisions – Freestyle Acrobatic Dough Tossing, Fastest Dough, Largest Dough Stretch, Fastest Pizza Box Folding, Pizza Triathlon, and Tandem Team Competition!

  • ITALIAN SANDWICH CHAMPION
    -ship

    The International Italian Sandwich Competition is back for a 3rd year in 2023 and will take place on the show floor in the IPC area. The competition will be limited to the first 30 entrants presenting a sandwich seen on the menu of a pizzeria, Italian restaurant, or bakery.

  • The Largest U.S. Pizza-Making Competition

    Since its inception in 2007, the International Pizza Challenge™ has evolved and grown in stature and prize money to become the most prestigious pizza-making competition in North America, if not the world. This year, we’ll have four divisions: traditional, Traditional, Non-Traditional, Pan, and Neapolitan/STG which will be represented in the pizza bake-off that will showcase 200 of the world’s best pizza makers.

  • California-Style Competition

    This year at International Pizza Expo 2023, we’ll be searching for the best non-traditional California-Style pizza using fresh dough with any combination of toppings and Real California Cheese. The 2023 California-Style International Pizza Competition will have two (2) divisions, Amateur/Student and Professional divisions with a maximum of 15 competitors in each division for a total of 30 competitors from across Southeast Asia and Mexico.

  • BEER & BULL NETWORKING

    BAfter the Exhibit Hall closes and the afternoon seminars conclude, the Beer & Bull early-evening session provides networkers and problem solvers with the opportunity to meet like-minded pizza professionals in a relaxed setting. Informal discussions on any pizza business topic take place at the round tables in the large Beer & Bull room, and all registered attendees are invited for free beer, snacks and other refreshments.
